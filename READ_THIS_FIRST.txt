                         HOW TO USE THE APP
================================================================================

1. Click "Select Folder to Clean"
2. Choose your Downloads folder
3. Pick "Oldest First" sorting
4. Swipe through files:
   - RIGHT arrow or D key = Keep
   - LEFT arrow or A key  = Delete (goes to trash)
   - UP arrow or W key    = Skip
5. View your cleanup stats at the end!
